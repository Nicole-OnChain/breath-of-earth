## Content Leverage System (Codex-Automated)

**Core principle:** Every piece of content gets leveraged to its fullest capacity. Never create once, post once.

### Breath of Earth Pipeline (per episode)
1. Produce episode in Venice Studio
2. **TikTok:** Full episode post
3. **Instagram Reels:** Same video, different hashtags
4. **YouTube Shorts:** Same video
5. **Facebook:** Post in romantasy/fantasy groups, share on timeline
6. **Etsy:** Extract key frames → coloring pages (PDF), art prints, wallpaper packs, lore guide chapters
7. **Amazon KDP:** Full coloring book (30-50 illustrations collected across episodes)
8. **App:** Full episode (paywalled or free preview)
9. **Engagement posts:** Character art, "next episode" countdowns, polls ("What should Lyra do?"), lore drops, behind-the-scenes, production stills
10. **Cross-pollination:** TikTok Shop ads for Etsy products, Etsy products link back to TikTok

### Crypto City Pipeline (per video)
1. Record/edit YouTube video
2. **YouTube:** Full video
3. **TikTok:** 3-5 clips per video
4. **Instagram Reels:** Same clips
5. **Facebook:** Share in crypto groups
6. **Newsletter:** Written segment around the topic
7. **Educational infographics:** Key data visualized
8. **Course modules:** Built from recurring topics

- Reels: Same videos as TikTok, different hashtags

- Stories: "Making of" content, episode countdowns, audience engagement
- Hashtags: #romantasy #booktok #darkromance #enemiestolovers #fantasytiktok #breathofearth

### TikTok (Primary — Daily Content)
- @breathofearth handle
- Daily posting at launch (7PM ET weekdays, 9AM ET weekends)
- 3 episodes/week + 2-4 teasers/engagement posts
- Reply to EVERY comment within 1 hour
- Engage with other romantasy/BookTok creators
- TikTok Shop ads: Heavy first 3 months, then throttle back
- Cross-pollinate: TikTok ads for Etsy products → Etsy products link back to TikTok

### YouTube (Secondary — Longer Revenue Tail)
- @breathofearth for BoE clips (Shorts)
- @NicoleOnChain for Crypto City (full videos)
- Monetize at 1K subs + 10M Shorts views (harder threshold but longer revenue)

### Etsy (Digital Products — Day 1)
- BreathOfEarthShop handle
- **Start digital only:** Stock photos, art prints, wallpaper packs, lore guides (PDFs)
- **Later:** Sticker packs per season (physical, mailed from home)
- Pricing: $3-8 digital, $8-15 physical stickers
- Cross-pollinate: TikTok Shop ads → Etsy products → back to TikTok

### Amazon KDP (Physical — Print on Demand)
- Amazon handles manufacturing and shipping. No inventory.
- Coloring books: 30-50 illustrations, $6.99-9.99
- Launch 2-4 weeks after TikTok launch
- "Breath of Earth" as brand name on Amazon

### The App (PWA — Launch MVP, Polish Later)
- **Don't overbuild this.** Start with a PWA (progressive web app) — works like an app but runs in browser, no app store needed
- Free tier: Episode previews, lore snippets, character profiles
- Paid: Full episodes ad-free ($0.99-1.99 each), seasons ($7.99-12.99)
- This can be a Codex + Mia job, but it's a SEPARATE project — don't let it delay launch
- If it's not ready by launch day, launch without it. Add it later.

### The Website (breathofearthseries.com — The Hub)
- **This is the stable hub.** Everything points here. TikTok bio, Instagram bio, Facebook, Etsy, Amazon — all link here.
- Pages: Home (hero video + CTA), Episodes, World (lore + characters), Shop (links to Etsy + Amazon), App, Subscribe (email list)
- Tech: Shopify (built-in store, scales, app integration)
- Must be live, nice, and functional BEFORE first TikTok post
- This is where people land when they want to find everything
