## Weinstein Stage Analysis (Core Method)
- **Stage 1 (BASING):** Accumulation zone, price flat below 30WMA → wait for breakout
- **Stage 2 (ADVANCING):** Price above 30WMA, uptrend → buy on pullbacks
- **Stage 3 (TOPPING):** Price far above 30WMA, momentum fading → prepare exit
- **Stage 4 (DECLINING):** Price below falling 30WMA → EXIT or avoid
- **30WMA** = 30-week moving average (key support/resistance)
