## Tax Reserve

**Why separate:** Creative income is 1099 income — no employer withholding. Every dollar earned needs tax set aside or you get a surprise bill.

**Estimated tax rate on self-employment income:** ~25-30% federal + state
- Self-employment tax: 15.3% (Social Security + Medicare)
- Federal income tax (bracket-dependent): 10-22%
- State: depends on filing state
- **Conservative estimate: set aside 30% for taxes**

**But the 5% line item is ON TOP of the revenue split above.** Here's how it actually works:

| Revenue | Allocation | % | Notes |
|---------|-----------|---|-------|
| Gross | Tax reserve | 30% | Set aside before any other allocation |
| Remaining 70% | Production | 30% of 70% | |
| Remaining 70% | Reinvestment | 40% of 70% | |
| Remaining 70% | Portfolio | 15% of 70% | Feeds DCA targets |
| Remaining 70% | Reserve | 10% of 70% | Emergency fund |
| Remaining 70% | Buffer | 5% of 70% | Overages, platform fees |

**Example:** $1,000 revenue month
- $300 → tax reserve (set aside, do not touch until quarterly payment)
- $210 → production costs
- $280 → reinvestment
- $105 → portfolio DCA
- $70 → reserve
- $35 → buffer

**Tax payments:** Quarterly estimated payments (April 15, June 15, Sept 15, Jan 15)
- Use Schedule C for business income/expenses
- Track all business expenses (Venice Studio credits, Shopify, Etsy fees, domain, tools) — they reduce taxable income
- The tax engine in `vault/tax.md` can handle this income stream

**Important:** All Breath of Earth revenue is taxable as self-employment income. All Breath of Earth expenses (production tools, subscriptions, fees) are deductible business expenses. Track everything from day one.

## Key Principles
- **Everything built before the first post** — don't go viral with nowhere to send people
- **Owned platforms first** — website and email list are yours; TikTok and Instagram are rented
- **Digital products before physical** — lower overhead, instant delivery, no inventory
- **Per-episode pricing is impulse-buy range** — $0.99-1.99 per episode, $7.99-12.99 per season
- **The app exists to own the audience** — no algorithm can take it away
- **Cross-post everything** — one video, five platforms, zero extra production cost
