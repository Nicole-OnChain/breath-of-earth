## AI Content Rules (Critical)
- TikTok **allows** AI-generated content ✅
- TikTok **requires** clear labeling for realistic AI media
- TikTok does **NOT reward** "low-value automation, copied media, deceptive synthetic media, or repetitive template spam"
- **What works:** Original series, consistent characters, strong narration, rights-safe audio
- **What gets demonetized:** Mass-produced template content, unedited AI dumps
- **Our content qualifies** — original characters, original story, voiceover narration, curated production

## The Numbers: How Many Videos to Monetization?

### Conservative Path (Realistic for New Account)
- Need: 10K followers + 100K views/month
- Average romantasy TikTok video: 2,000-10,000 views for new accounts
- A single viral video can hit 100K-1M+ views
- **Realistic timeline: 2-4 months** with consistent posting

### Content Vault Strategy (Nicole's Approach — SMART)
- Build ALL content before launching
- Never miss a posting day because you're "still making it"
- Drop episodes while audience momentum is hot
- Avoid the creator death spiral: post → wait → scramble → post late → lose momentum

### How Many Videos Do You Need Pre-Built?

**To guarantee 10K followers and 100K monthly views:**

Assume:
- Each episode gets 3,000-8,000 views in first week (new account)
- 1 in 5 episodes hits "mini-viral" (50K-200K views)
- Conversion rate to followers: 2-5% of viewers
- Posting frequency: 1 episode per day, 5 days/week

**The math:**
- 20 episodes posted over 4 weeks = 60K-160K total views (some will re-watch)
- 1-4 mini-viral episodes push total past 100K/month threshold
- 2-5% follower conversion on 100K views = 2,000-5,000 followers
- Need 2-3 months of consistent posting to hit 10K followers
- OR: 1 truly viral episode (500K+ views) can hit 10K followers in a week

**Content vault needed: 30-45 episodes** (covers 6-9 weeks of 5x/week posting)
**That's approximately 3 seasons of Breath of Earth** (11 eps × 3 seasons = 33 episodes)

### Recommended Post Frequency: DAILY at launch, then 5-6x/week
- **Launch phase (first 5 weeks):** Post every single day — algorithm rewards consistency
- **Weekdays:** 7:00 PM ET (post-work scrolling peak)
- **Weekends:** 9:00 AM ET (weekend morning peak — Buffer's #1 slot)
- **After hitting 10K followers:** Can drop to 5-6x/week
- **Rest day (if needed):** Wednesday — lowest engagement day

### Episode Release Rhythm (Launch Phase — Daily)
- **Daily:** One content piece per day, alternating between episodes and engagement
- **Episode days:** Full episode drops (main content)
- **Between-episode days:** Teasers, character art, "last time on" recaps, polls, lore drops, behind-the-scenes
- **The daily cliffhanger is the hook** — "what happens next?" only works if next is tomorrow
- **Teaser content ideas:** Character reference art, "next episode" countdown, poll "what should Lyra do?", world-building lore drops, production bloopers, "making of" shorts

### Vault Math (Daily Posting)
- 33 episodes ÷ 7/week = ~5 weeks of main content
- Plus 10-15 teasers filling gap days
- That's enough to hit 10K followers and 100K monthly views if 2-3 episodes go mini-viral
- After 10K followers: slow to 5-6x/week, sustain on backlog while producing new eps
- Always keep 2+ weeks of content in the vault
