# Mom and Dad — Character Profile

**Novel:** Breath of Earth — Book 1
**Role:** Memory. Home. What Lyra lost.
**Element:** None (normals)
**First appearance:** Chapter 3 (The Sister) — flashback only

---

## Physical Description
- **Mom:** Silver-blonde hair like Lyra, warm smile, simple clothing
- **Dad:** Dark hair, kind smile, strong hands

## Who They Are in Book 1
They only exist in Lyra's memory. A warm kitchen. A full table. Four kids and two parents who loved them without condition.

They didn't know about elements either. None of the kids did. It was a happy, normal family in the middle of nowhere. Until it wasn't.

## What They Represent
Home. Safety. Unconditional love. The thing Lyra is fighting to get back to.

Not complicated. Not conflicted. Just warm. Just gone.

## Writing Them
- Keep them brief. One memory. One feeling. Don't over-explain.
- The warmth of the memory is the point. The contrast with the cold hierarchy is what matters.
- No dialogue needed. Just the feeling of a kitchen, a table, a family.
