# The Twins (Red-Headed Brothers) — Character Profile

**Novel:** Breath of Earth — Book 1
**Role:** Background characters. Seeds.
**Element:** Fire (unmanifested in Book 1)
**Age:** ~8 years old
**First appearance:** Chapter 3 (The Sister) — flashback, sleeping beside Lyra

---

## Physical Description
- Identical red-headed boys
- Freckled, wild, mischievous
- Simple worn clothing
- Always together. Always moving.

## Who They Are in Book 1
They're not characters yet. They're warmth. They're the reason Lyra is fighting. Two little boys sleeping beside her in a memory, and she's thinking "No one will hurt them."

That's it. That's enough.

## What They Become (Later Seasons)
Fire elements. The family pattern: Wind (Lyra), Nothing (Sera), Fire (twins). Everyone has something. Everyone except Sera.

The twins deepen Sera's isolation — even the little kids have something coming. Everyone except her.

## Writing the Twins in Book 1
- Don't name them yet. Don't explain them. Just show they exist.
- A flash of red hair. Two small bodies beside Lyra in the dark. That's all the reader needs.
- They're not plot. They're motivation. They're home.
