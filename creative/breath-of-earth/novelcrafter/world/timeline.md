# Breath of Earth — Book 1 Timeline

**Novel:** Breath of Earth — Book 1 (Season 1 Novella)
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-05-12

---

## Master Timeline

### Before the Story
- Lyra, Sera, and the twins grow up in a normal family. Happy. No one knows about elements.
- Mom and Dad love all four kids equally. Warm home. Middle of nowhere.
- Sera has no element. The boys will have Fire (unmanifested).
- Lyra's element is dormant. She doesn't know it exists.

### Night 1: The Party (Chapter 1)
- Lyra is at a college party. She doesn't want to be there.
- A guy corners her. "You don't belong here."
- Her power erupts. Wind explosion. Drinks fly, people scatter.
- Ronan appears. Steps between her and the threat. No words.
- His hand on her back. Sparks where they touch.
- They walk into the night. She follows.

### Night 1-2: The Walk / The Escape (Chapter 2)
- They walk through dark streets toward the outskirts.
- He doesn't speak. She doesn't ask questions.
- She's cold. He doesn't offer warmth. The ground shifts under her feet anyway — not helping, not not helping.
- Dawn approaches. She stumbles. He catches her arm, lets go immediately.
- They reach the edge of the city. Trees. He says one word: "Keep up."

### Day 2: The Promise (Chapter 3)
- Lyra asks about her family. He goes still.
- **Flashback:** Home. Warm kitchen. Two red-headed twins sleeping beside her. Mom and Dad. Sera in the doorway, watching. Not warm. Not grateful. Something off.
- Back in present: Lyra tells Ronan about Sera. About the twins. About her parents.
- He listens. Then he reaches out and grabs her chin. Firm. Not rough. Steadies her.
- "No one will hurt them."
- His first real words to her. A promise.
- In the shadows, Sera watches. Or — the memory of her watching. A seed planted.

### Night 2-3: The Night (Chapter 4)
- Cold night. They're still walking. No shelter yet.
- She falls asleep against a tree. He sits across from her, keeping watch.
- In her sleep, she shivers. The air around her pulls cold.
- He doesn't decide to help. His body does. He shifts closer. Warmth radiates from him. His arm relaxes in sleep.
- She wakes in the morning. He's already up, already walking away.
- "Like it was my fault." — She's annoyed he helped. More annoyed she noticed.

### Day 3: The River / The Arrival (Chapter 5)
- They reach a river. Caspian is there. Dark-skinned, light eyes, water mist around his shoulders.
- "Watch your step." — It's about the river. It's also about everything.
- Caspian assesses her. Wind-weaver. He sees what she is.
- They reach the camp. Ronan leaves immediately. Walks down toward the bottom.
- Caspian watches her watching Ronan go. "You'll want to follow him. Don't."

### Day 4-5: The Camp (Chapter 6)
- Zara recruits Lyra. Flame-red hair, amber eyes, warm and sharp. "Oh, you'll do."
- Zara gives her the tower tour. Prissy, sarcastic, genuine. "Better air flow. Obviously."
- The tour goes down: Wind Tower → Volcano-side → Mid-level → Ground level.
- At the bottom, Lyra sees Ronan carrying stones in the Break Yard. He doesn't look up.
- Zara pulls her away. "You don't go down there."
- From the tower, Lyra can see the whole camp. She can see where he is. She can see where she is. The distance is the hierarchy made physical.

### Day 6-7: The Hierarchy (Chapter 7)
- Zara takes Lyra to the Scorch Ring. Inside the volcano. Fire's home turf.
- "Told you. Advantage." Zara knocks her flat.
- Lyra gets up. Rolls to the edge. Looks down through the caldera.
- Below: Ronan carrying stones. Break Yard. Bottom of everything.
- Zara pulls her back. "Focus."
- Wind picks up around Lyra. Not commanded. Responding to something she's feeling.
- The hierarchy is not abstract. She can see it. She can feel it. And the wind knows.

### Day 8-9: The Explosion (Chapter 8)
- Training intensifies. Lyra's power grows but so does her frustration.
- She's learning to use wind, but she's learning something else too — the hierarchy is wrong. Not because someone told her. Because she can see it.
- She sees Ronan from above. He's always working. Never training. Just labor.
- An explosion of wind — not controlled, not commanded. The wind had other ideas.
- She looks down at the Break Yard. He looks up.
- That's all. One look. But she felt it.

### Day 9-10: The Triangle (Chapter 9)
- Enforcers come for Lyra. The system is closing in.
- Ronan steps between. Raises a rock wall. Protects her.
- He's taken. Bound. Led away. One look up at her.
- Zara grabs her arm. "You'll make it worse."
- Lyra stands in the Scorch Ring. Wind picking up. Not rage. Something quieter. Decided.
- "He saved me. They took him for it. I'm getting him back."

### Day 10: The Pit (Chapter 10)
- Lyra descends through the camp. From the tower to the bottom.
- Caspian is waiting at the Drown Pit. He points down at the Break Yard. "That's where they keep them."
- The Break Yard. The Cell. Iron bars. Squalor.
- Ronan inside. Punishment marks on his body. He doesn't look up when she arrives.
- Her hand on the bars. His hand shifts toward hers. Not touching. Just... closer.
- That was enough.

### Day 10-11: The Rescue (Chapter 11)
- Lyra goes back to the Break Yard. Wind storm across the camp — a distraction.
- She reaches the cell. His hand is already reaching through the bars.
- He speaks for the first time: "You shouldn't be here."
- Five words. Every word he never said.
- Earth clears the path. Not commanded. Not controlled. The ground moves for him like the air moves for her.
- They run through the jungle. Hands finding each other.
- Lyra looks back at the camp. She doesn't know where they're going. She knows she's not going back.
