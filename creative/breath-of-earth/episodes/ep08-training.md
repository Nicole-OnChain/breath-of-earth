## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

Lyra trains with Zara in the Scorch Ring. Fire vs wind. Zara is better — more controlled, more skilled. Their elements clash and it's electric. Caspian intervenes with water, effortlessly separating them. Later, alone at night, Lyra practices her wind. Ronan finds her. He tries to walk past. His hand almost touches her face. Stops. Pulls back. A rock wall slams between them. He walks away. She's left alone with the wind barely moving.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

### Zara (Flame-kissed)
She's training Lyra — and she's better. Not just more powerful. More controlled. She's been doing this for years. Their elements clash and it's electric, but Zara isn't threatened. She's amused. Competitive. She pushes Lyra because that's how you get stronger.

### Caspian (Tide-caller)
He intervenes when fire and wind get too intense. Water between them. Effortless. He doesn't take sides — he just stops it. Where Zara is fire, Caspian is still water. Both of them watch Lyra. For different reasons.

### Ronan
He finds her at night. Practicing alone. He tries to walk past. His hand moves toward her face — almost touches her. Then stops. A rock wall slams between them. He walks away. The wall is his reflex. His armor. But his hand moved first. That's the crack.
