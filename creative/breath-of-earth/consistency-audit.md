### Voiceover Style Consistency
- All episodes use Lyra as single narrator
- Short, punchy lines. Never more than 2 sentences per beat.
- "She flew. Like a leaf." style — image does the work, voiceover is sparse
- Text overlays match key voiceover lines
- Consistent across all 9 episodes ✅

### Zara's Voice
- "Sweetheart," "princess," "honey" — consistently warm with an edge
- Ep 7: "You sleep like the dead, sweetheart" / "Don't blow us off the platform" / "Told you. Advantage."
- Ep 8: "Oh, you'll do" (warm AND calculating)
- Ep 9 v3.0: Enforcers come for Lyra. Ronan raises rock wall to protect her (active choice). One look between them. Zara grabs her in the Scorch Ring. "He saved me. They took him for it. I'm getting him back."
- Consistent across all appearances ✅

### The Wind Motif
- Ep 7: "The wind knew" — emotion leaking through
- Ep 8: "The wind had other ideas" — loss of control
- Ep 9: Wind as resolve — not rage, not explosion, a choice
- Ep 10 (outline): Wind as quiet decision — "something quieter. Decided."
- Ep 11 (outline): Wind as tool — distraction, not weapon
- Progressive arc ✅
