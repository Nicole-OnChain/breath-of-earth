## THE RULES

### World Rules
- Elemental hierarchy: Wind (top) > Fire > Water > Earth (bottom)
- Cross-element relationships are FORBIDDEN
- Wind-weavers live in the tower (top of the camp)
- Flame-kissed train in the Scorch Ring (volcanic caldera, upper level)
- Tide-callers train in the Drown Pit (sunken arena, mid-level)
- Earth-shapers labor in the Break Yard (mud and rock, ground level)
- Enforcers wear dark military-style uniforms, no element insignia
- The system punishes hierarchy violations harshly

### Voice Rules
- **Lyra:** Short, punchy, 1-2 sentences per beat. Sparse. Image does the work.
  - Pattern: "She flew. Like a leaf." / "One look. That's all I got."
  - Never over-explains. States what happened, lets the audience feel it.
- **Zara:** "Sweetheart," "princess," "honey" — warm with an edge
  - Ep 6: "Oh, you'll do" (warm AND calculating)
  - Ep 7: "You sleep like the dead, sweetheart" / "Don't blow us off the platform"
  - Ep 9: "You go down there, you'll make it worse" (explaining, not cruel)
- **Ronan:** Does NOT speak until Ep 11. FIVE WORDS: "You shouldn't be here."
- **Caspian:** Tight jaw, eyes say what his mouth won't. Minimal dialogue.

### Format Rules
- 9:16 vertical (TikTok + Reels)
- 85-100 seconds per episode
- 8-10 clips per episode
- Voiceover: Lyra (single narrator)
- Text overlays match key voiceover lines (italic in scripts)
- Hard cuts for impact moments, crossfades for transitions
- EXCEPT: hard cut before Ronan speaks (Ep 11)

## CHARACTERS

### Lyra (Wind-weaver, POV narrator)
- Silver-blonde hair, young, determined
- Wind responds to her emotions (Eps 7-8: explodes when suppressed)
- Ep 9: wind as resolve. Ep 10: wind as quiet decision. Ep 11: wind as tool
- Never over-explains. States what happened.
- Arc: suppress → explode → choose → rescue → lead

### Ronan (Earth-shaper, silent protector)
- Tall, muscular, brown and green clothing
- Does NOT speak until Ep 11. Five words: "You shouldn't be here."
- Earth responds to him — Ep 2 (flinches), Ep 9 (rock wall), Ep 11 (clears path)
- Punished for saving Lyra in Ep 9 — enforcers caught him using earth magic
- Carries the weight silently. Never complains. Never explains.

### Zara (Flame-kissed, frenemy)
- Red hair, confident, warm with an edge
- Calls Lyra "sweetheart," "princess," "honey"
- Ep 7: knocks Lyra flat ("Advantage"), grip on her arm
- Ep 9: grabs Lyra in the Scorch Ring — same grip, same hallway callback
- "You'll make it worse" — not cruel, just realistic about the system
- The kindness and the cage are the same gesture

### Caspian (Tide-caller, the triangle's third point)
- Dark skin, striking light eyes
- Ep 5: first appears (just a look at the Drown Pit)
- Ep 6: full interaction ("Don't trust the currents, Weaver")
- Ep 9: watches Ronan being taken, something cracks behind his eyes
- Ep 10: waits at Drown Pit for Lyra, points her to Ronan's cell
- He trained against Earth in the Break Yard — that's how he knows Ronan
- Can't go down there himself, but can show her where

### Sera (Lyra's twin sister)
- Identical face, no element, watches from shadows
- Cold calculation behind Lyra's warmth
- Ep 3: planted. Season 2 villain seed.

### The Twins (red-haired, young)
- Flash in Ep 3. Will become Fire. Season 2 seed.

### Enforcers
- Dark military-style uniforms, no element insignia
- Faces hard and expressionless — not people, function
- First appear in Ep 9
