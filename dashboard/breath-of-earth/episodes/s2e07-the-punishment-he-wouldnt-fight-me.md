## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

Lyra and Ronan are forced to fight each other in the Scorch Ring — the same ring where he saved her in S1. They spar at first, Ronan deflecting more than attacking. During the fight, he gets close and tells her — Zara led the guards to our hideout. Don't trust her. Then the uncle signals for the fight to end. Ronan drops his hands. Gets on his knees. Refuses to fight her. Lyra has to end it. She does a show move — a wind blast that throws him back. It looks brutal. It injures him badly. But she doesn't kill him. The uncle and Zara watch from the sidelines.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

### Ronan
He fights just enough to make it look real. Then he gets close enough to tell her the truth about Zara. Then he stops. He gets on his knees. He's not giving up — he's choosing her. He'd rather take the hit than raise a hand against her.

### Lyra
She has to hurt the person she loves. The wind blast she uses is supposed to look devastating. It is devastating. She injures him badly. But she doesn't kill him. She can't. And the uncle sees that.

### Zara
She watches from the sidelines. The audience thinks she's with the uncle. She's watching for a different reason — she needs to know if Lyra and Ronan are still together. If they're still strong. They are. Good.

### The Uncle
He watches to see if they'll break each other. They don't break. They choose each other. He files this away.
