## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

Guards breach the water cavern. There's a fight — all four of them fighting together for the first time. But they're outnumbered and outmaneuvered. The uncle walks in at the end and it's over before it starts. They're taken back to camp. Lyra sees the hierarchy's real face for the first time — not punishment. Control.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

### Lyra
She fights. Not gracefully — desperately. She's not trained for combat and it shows. But she fights.

### Ronan
He doesn't fight for himself. He fights to keep Lyra behind him. Earth walls between her and every guard that comes close.

### Caspian
Water moves like it's part of him. This is the first time we see him actually fight — and he's devastating. The Drown Pit trained him well.

### Zara
She fights alongside them — but she's the first to see it's hopeless. She stops before anyone else. Not because she's weak. Because she's already calculating.

### The Uncle
He doesn't fight. He walks in after. Calm. Like he knew they were here the whole time. Like this was inevitable.
