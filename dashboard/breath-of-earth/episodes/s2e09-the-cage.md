## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

Lyra is alone in the Wind tower. Zara has "moved up" — seen with the uncle, given privileges. Lyra doesn't trust her. But Zara slips a note under her door. Two words: "He's alive." Lyra doesn't know who — Ronan? She holds the note all night. The cage doesn't feel as empty.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

### Lyra
She's been alone. Combat training. Wind tower. No allies. No information. The note is the first thing that's felt real in days. She doesn't know what it means. But she doesn't let it go.

### Zara
The audience saw her take the uncle's pin in Ep 8. Now they see her slip a note to Lyra. Is it a trap? Is it genuine? Zara's expression gives nothing away. She's playing both sides — and the audience doesn't know which side is real yet.
