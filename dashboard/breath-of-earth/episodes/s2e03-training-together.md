## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

Lighter episode. Ronan and Lyra train against each other — wind vs earth — sparring to stay sharp. Caspian is the hilarious third wheel, sitting on the sidelines making commentary, calling out their obvious attraction, refusing to leave even when they ask. The training gets intense. Lyra and Ronan nearly kiss during a close sparring moment. Caspian loudly announces he's "still here" and breaks the tension. Ends with all three laughing around the pool — the last light moment before everything goes dark.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

### Lyra
She's training hard. She wants to be ready. But when she and Ronan spar, the tension isn't just combat. She notices. He notices. Caspian notices. She's not ready to name it.

### Ronan
He's a better fighter than he lets on. When they spar, he holds back — not because he can't win, but because he won't hit her full force. She calls him on it. He doesn't deny it.

### Caspian
He's the comic relief. He sits by the pool making commentary. "You two want me to leave? Too bad, this is my house." When they almost kiss, he says "STILL HERE" loudly and breaks the moment. He's having the best day he's had in years.
