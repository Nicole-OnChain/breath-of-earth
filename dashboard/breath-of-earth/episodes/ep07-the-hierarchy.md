## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

The hierarchy becomes physical. Lyra trains with Zara in the Scorch Ring — fire vs wind, and Zara is better. Then she looks down at the Break Yard where Ronan carries stones alone. The episode is structured around four element trainings, each showing a different level. Air at the top. Fire below. Water further down. Earth at the bottom. And Ronan is Earth.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

### Zara (Flame-kissed)
She's training Lyra. Not because she's nice. Because a Wind-weaver without control is dangerous. She's competitive, sharp, and genuinely skilled. When their elements clash, it's electric — but Lyra can't match her. Not yet. Zara's not threatened. She's amused.

### Ronan
He doesn't appear until the end. When we see him, he's carrying stones in the Break Yard. Not using his power. Just labor. That's what Earth-shapers do at the bottom. The man who can make the earth move is carrying rocks by hand.
