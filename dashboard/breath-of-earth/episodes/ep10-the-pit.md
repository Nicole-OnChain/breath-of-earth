## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

Lyra can't sleep. She descends through the entire camp — Wind tower → Scorch Ring → Drown Pit → Break Yard — each level showing more of what the hierarchy does to people. Caspian intercepts her at the Drown Pit. He doesn't stop her. He shows her where Ronan is. She finds him in a rough stone cell. Punishment marks on his arms. He doesn't look up. Her hand through the bars. His hand moves toward hers. Not all the way. Just toward.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

### Lyra
She's not storming in. She's quiet and determined. The wind around her isn't rage or resolve — it's something quieter. Something that's decided.

### Caspian
He's waiting at the Drown Pit. Like he knew she'd come. He doesn't try to stop her — he helps. He points. Down. His jaw is tight. He can't go down there, but he can show her where.

### Ronan
He doesn't look up. He doesn't expect anyone to come. His hand moving toward Lyra's — not all the way, just toward — is the whole emotional arc.
