## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

Zara unlocks Lyra's tower from within. Zara unlocks the disciplinary cells. Zara gets Ronan out. The camp is in chaos — Zara's intel triggered an attack from outside by former escapees. Lyra, Ronan, and Zara breach the camp wall as the hierarchy's enforcers scramble. They don't win. They don't defeat the system. They get out.
