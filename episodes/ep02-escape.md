## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

**The car (Clip 1):** Suffocating. She's rigid. He's gripping the wheel like he wants to break it. No explanation. No destination. Just drive.

**The pullover (Clip 2):** He yanks the door. Doesn't wait. Doesn't look back. And then — the earth recoils from his anger. This is Ronan's power on display for the first time. Not gentle. Not controlled. The ground flinches.

**The fall (Clip 3):** She crashes. He looks down at her like she's in his way. Not concern. Impatience. This is who he is right now.

**The binding (Clip 4):** He fixes her. Efficient. Fast. No warmth. "Like fixing a leak." But he does it. He couldn't not.

**The touch (Clip 5):** Sparks. Both of them shocked. The same spark from Episode 1. It's not romantic — it's electric. And it terrifies him.

**The shutter (Clip 6):** He yanks his hand back. Expression slams shut. Walks ahead. Doesn't look back. "Keep up." The warmth from the binding is gone. The wall is back up. But the audience knows: he felt something. And he's running from it.
