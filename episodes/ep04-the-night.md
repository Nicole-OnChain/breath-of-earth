## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

**The whole episode is the night.** After the chin grab and the promise, they have to sleep in the same space. He made camp. She's freezing. He doesn't want to care. He does.

**Clip 3 (the warmth) is the crux:** He pulls her against him roughly. His arms are stiff. He's mad about it. But he did it. He couldn't not. This is NOT a romantic moment — it's reluctant and uncomfortable and neither of them wants to be there. But his body heat saves her from freezing. That's it. That's all it is. And that's enough.

**Clip 4 (the shift) is the crack:** His arm relaxes in his sleep. Not holding her. Just... there. His body made a choice his mind wouldn't. The first sign that the wall has a door.

**Clip 5 (the morning) is the punch:** She wakes up and he's looking at her. Not warm. Not cold. Just *seeing* her. The first time. He's been watching her sleep. He didn't have to. But he was.

**Clip 6 (the wall) is the slam:** He stands up. Walks away. Cold again. Like it never happened. Because admitting it happened would mean admitting he felt something. And he can't do that yet.

**The whole episode is a tension arc:** Cold → reluctant warmth → tiny crack → real seeing → wall back up. The audience should feel like they almost got something real, and then he took it away.
