## WHAT THIS EPISODE DOES

Aftermath of the wind blast. Enforcers come for Lyra — she threw a supervisor across the Scorch Ring. They grab her. Ronan feels it through the earth. A rock wall rises between her and the enforcers. Everyone knows where it came from. They take him. He doesn't fight. One look. That's all she gets. Zara pulls Lyra back. "You'll make it worse." The episode ends with Lyra standing alone. The wind picks up. Not rage. Resolve.

---

## CHARACTER NOTES

### Ronan (Earth-shaper)
He felt it. The ground carries everything — sound, vibration, distress. He didn't have to do anything. He could have stayed in the mud. He chose to act. And that act — earth moving where it shouldn't — is all the evidence they need.

### Caspian (Tide-caller)
He's training in the Drown Pit when the ground trembles. He knows what earth magic feels like. He knows Ronan. Something cracks behind his eyes.

### Zara (Flame-kissed)
She stops Lyra from going after Ronan. "You'll make it worse." Not cruel. Realistic. But Ronan already took the hit. Zara's keeping her from making it worse for HIM.
