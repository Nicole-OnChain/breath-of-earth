### Lessons Learned
- TAO Stats free tier is very restrictive — 10K credits/month goes fast
- Must cache aggressively and avoid re-fetching
- Pool history pagination can be 86+ pages per subnet — limit to 2 pages (200 days)
- The subnet endpoint returns 100 items per page — need 2 pages for all 129
- Rate limits apply across all endpoints simultaneously
- Chart generation works great with matplotlib (dark theme, MAs, price line)
